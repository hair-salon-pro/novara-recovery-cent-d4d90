Frequently Asked Questions About Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP)

How much does Novara Recovery Center's Intensive Outpatient Program (IOP) cost, and do you accept insurance?
The cost of our Intensive Outpatient Program can vary depending on the length of treatment and individual needs. We are in-network with several major insurance providers and also accept out-of-network benefits. Our team is happy to verify your insurance coverage and discuss any out-of-pocket costs before you begin the program. For those without insurance, we offer flexible self-pay options. Please contact us directly for a confidential assessment and a personalized cost estimate.
How long does the IOP last at Novara Recovery Center, and what is the weekly commitment?
Our IOP typically lasts 8 to 12 weeks, but the exact duration may be adjusted based on your progress and clinical needs. Participants generally attend group therapy sessions 3 to 5 days a week, with each session lasting about 3 hours. We also schedule individual counseling and family support sessions as part of your treatment plan. We offer both daytime and evening groups to accommodate work and family commitments common in the Merrifield and greater Fairfax area.
Is Novara Recovery Center licensed and accredited to provide addiction treatment in Virginia?
Yes, Novara Recovery Center is fully licensed by the Virginia Department of Behavioral Health and Developmental Services (DBHDS). Our facility meets all state and local regulations for outpatient addiction treatment. In addition, our clinicians are credentialed and experienced in providing evidence-based care for substance use disorders.
What is the intake and assessment process like for new clients at your Merrifield location?
When you reach out to us, we’ll start with a confidential phone consultation to understand your situation and answer your initial questions. If you choose to move forward, we’ll schedule an in-person or virtual assessment at our Merrifield office. During this assessment, our clinical team will review your substance use history, medical background, and recovery goals, and work with you to develop a personalized treatment plan. Most clients are able to begin IOP within a few days of their assessment, depending on availability.
Do you guarantee sobriety after completing the IOP at Novara Recovery Center?
While we are committed to providing the highest quality care and support, we cannot guarantee sobriety after program completion. Recovery is a lifelong process that requires ongoing effort and support. We do, however, equip our clients with the tools, resources, and aftercare planning needed for continued success. Many clients continue with our outpatient or alumni support services after completing IOP to maintain their recovery journey.
